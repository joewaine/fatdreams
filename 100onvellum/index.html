100 flourescent color pencil drawings on vellum

"I created this series of drawings over the course of 18 months from 2014-2016 while living in Oakland and Berkeley. I was obsessed with the combination of these color pencils, especially the white and yellow and blue and carmine and the slightly lifted effect that they have when done on vellum and scanned into the computer. Many of these originals are gone but I thought it would be cool to sell high quality 6x8" prints of them to help raise more money for charity. $5 each or 3 for $10 + shipping. Please email me with inquiries."
- j. waine
